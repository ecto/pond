KOI (Kinematic Onboard Inference) is the AI heart of Pond.

* **koi0-act** – 40 M-param INT8 reflex VLA (< 1.5 ms latency).
* **koi0-think** – 7 B Gemma-3 INT4 planner (≈ 90 ms).
  Early-exit keeps both in one TensorRT engine.
